Burti - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 3, 2026

1. Overview

Burti is a personal content automation tool. It is not a public service and does not collect personal data from any third-party users.

2. Data Collection

This application does not collect, store, or process any personal data from TikTok users or any other third parties. The only data accessed is the creator's own TikTok account information required for content publishing via the TikTok API.

3. Data Usage

OAuth tokens obtained during authorization are stored locally on the creator's device and are used exclusively to upload original video content to the creator's own TikTok account (@burti.kim).

4. Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third parties. No analytics, tracking, or advertising services are used.

5. Data Retention

OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be revoked at any time by the account owner. No other data is retained.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact: burti.kim (at) TikTok / Instagram
